Importance of Books

Books are a valuable source of knowledge, inspiration, and imagination. They help us learn new things, understand different perspectives, and improve our thinking skills. Reading books can reduce stress, expand vocabulary, and even transport us to different worlds. Whether for study or leisure, books play an important role in personal growth and lifelong learning.
These are some of my favourite books
  • The Stationary Shop of Tehran by Marjan Kamali
    A touching story of love and loss set in 1950s Iran.



  • As Long As The Lemon Trees Grow by Zoulfa Katouh
    A powerful story of hope, survival, and love in the midst of Syria’s civil war.



  • A Thousand Splendid Suns by Khaled Hosseini
    A deeply emotional story of two Afghan women bound by hardship, friendship, and resilience under a brutal regime.



  • Before the Coffee gets Cold by Toshikazu Kawaguchi
    A heartwarming tale of time travel, regrets, and second chances set in a small Tokyo café.



  • Atomic Habits by James clear
    A practical guide to building good habits, breaking bad ones, and making small changes that lead to big results.
